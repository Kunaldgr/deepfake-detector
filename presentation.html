Our Solution: ResNeXt-50 Architecture

Why ResNeXt-50?

  • 50 Layers - Deep hierarchical learning
  • Cardinality - 32 parallel feature paths
  • Residual Connections - Better gradient flow
  • Pre-trained - ImageNet transfer learning
  • Efficient - Better accuracy than ResNet-50

Training Details

  • 60,000+ images (50/50 real/fake split)
  • Adam optimizer, 0.0001 learning rate
  • Data augmentation for robustness
  • 99.89% training accuracy achieved
